The grid to the right will display your current location in the world. In general, green represents forest, yellow/orange represents plains, purple represents wetland, and blue represents water.

Tips:
  • The forests are safe, but bigger game lives on the plains.
  • Getting too close to water can be dangerous if you're young!
  • Don't spend too much time in one spot or else an enemy might pounce on you.
